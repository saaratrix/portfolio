WebGL watery ripple reveal effect

When the pointer moves the pointer position is stored and then increases the area of influence over time revealing more of the image. After some time the area of influence shrinks until it's gone.
Used jquery.ripples plugin to create the ripples.
A blur brush image was used to draw on a canvas to create a masking image to show the underlying image.
The project was previously found at mcdbooks.com but they've updated their landing page since then.
